Our Features

Dynamic Pricing Model

Our real-time AI-driven pricing system adjusts freelancer rates based on:

  • Market demand (e.g., SEO services in high demand increase pricing).
  • Freelancer performance (e.g., better ratings, faster delivery = higher pay).
  • Project complexity (e.g., advanced video editing costs more).

This ensures fair pricing for both clients and freelancers, improving market efficiency.

Freelancer Collaboration Pods & Agency Formation

Freelancers can team up to form small agencies (pods) for large-scale projects. Features include:

  • Team formation: A group of freelancers can work together on complex projects.
  • Clients can hire an entire pod for better coordination.
  • Revenue sharing system that splits earnings automatically.

This encourages collaboration, helps freelancers win bigger projects, and gives clients an end-to-end solution.

Verified AI-Powered Work Quality & Authenticity Checker

AI-powered tools to scan freelancer submissions for:

  • Plagiarism detection ensuring original content.
  • AI-generated content analysis preventing low-quality spam.
  • Grammar, SEO, and design checks to enhance work quality.

This builds trust and transparency while helping freelancers improve their work.

Milestone - Based Payments with Auto - Approval Timer

Clients can approve payments in milestones, with auto-approval timer:

  • Clients approve payments at milestones (e.g., 25% after initial draft, etc.).
  • If no response within a set time (e.g., 7 days), the milestone is auto-approved.

This ensures timely payments, reduces disputes, and smoothens the workflow.
